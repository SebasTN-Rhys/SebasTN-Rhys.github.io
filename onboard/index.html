What you get

Everything a business needs to accept payments without touching Stripe's API directly.

Global card payments

Accept Visa, Mastercard, Amex, and local payment methods in over 135 currencies. Stripe's checkout converts for you.

KYC & identity verification

Stripe handles all know-your-customer checks, ID verification, and business document collection. You don't touch any PII.

Automatic payouts

Funds hit your bank account on a daily schedule. No manual payout requests, no minimums.

1099-K filing

Stripe issues 1099-K forms automatically for US merchants who meet the threshold. Zero paperwork for you.

Platform fee: 2%

Sebastn takes a flat 2% platform fee on each processed charge. No monthly minimums, no setup fees.

Merchant dashboard

View your account status, recent payouts, and charge history at sebastn.com/m/{your-id} — secured with GitHat.

How it works

Four steps from zero to live.

  1. 1
    Create a GitHat account

    Sign in (or up) at githat.io — your single identity across all Sebastn apps.

  2. 2
    Tell us about your business

    Legal name, country, contact email, and a short description of what you sell.

  3. 3
    Complete Stripe KYC

    Stripe walks you through identity and bank verification on their hosted page. Sebastn never stores your SSN or bank details.

  4. 4
    Go live

    Your merchant dashboard activates. Start accepting payments through any Sebastn-powered checkout.
